About Krishi Salahkar

Revolutionizing agriculture in Odisha through AI-powered insights

Our Mission

To empower farmers in Odisha with AI-driven agricultural insights that help optimize crop yields, reduce risks, and improve livelihoods through data-driven decision making.

Key Features

Yield Prediction

Advanced ML models predict crop yields based on multiple factors including weather, soil, and historical data.

Personalized Recommendations

Tailored advice for irrigation, fertilization, and pest management based on your specific farm conditions.

District-Specific Data

Localized insights for all 30 districts of Odisha with region-specific agricultural practices.

Multi-Crop Support

Covers major crops including rice, maize, wheat, groundnut, cotton, sugarcane, and more.

Seasonal Advisory

Kharif, Rabi, and Zaid season-specific guidance with optimal timing recommendations for planting and harvesting.
